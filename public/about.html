What is Autism ?

Autism or autism spectrum disorder (ASD) is a developmental disorder It affects how people communicate and interact with others, as well as how they behave and learn.

Autism signs

Avoiding eye contact, weak communication skills, Unexpected reactions to sounds, tastes, sights,Difficulty understanding other people's emotions

How to deal with ASD?

Be empathetic , Make them feel safe and loved , Eliminate punishments , Be consistent , Make time for fun , Make time for fun Reward good behavior

Advice for educators

  1. Avoid sensory overload. Many unexpected things can be distracting to students with autism
  2. Use visuals
  3. Be predictable
  4. Keep language concrete
  5. Directly teach social skills

A Parent's Guide to Autism

  1. Signs of autism are not clear, or specific, in infancy
  2. There are different patterns of development in children with autism
  3. Children with autism may seem socially distant, but they do love their families
  4. Special, focused interests are common for people with autism
  5. Many people with autism experience anxiety
